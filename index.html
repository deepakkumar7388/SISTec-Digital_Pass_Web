Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the gate pass guidelines.

To ensure safety, a student's request is first evaluated by their Tutor Guardian (Level 1 TG). Once the TG signs off, the request goes to the HOD (Level 2) for final clearance. Once approved by the HOD, the approved pass is instantly synced to the guard terminal list for face verification.

The system defines geographical center coordinates for the campus gate. When guards verify passes, the app checks that the guard's device GPS lies within a 300-meter radius, securing checkouts from remote tampering.

When a visitor arrives, the guard registers their name and target host department. The system pushes a socket alert to the target faculty's device. Once the host accepts the meeting (marks as MEET), the visitor is allowed in.
